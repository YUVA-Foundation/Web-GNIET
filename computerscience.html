Computer Science Department____

Department has young & highly motivated faculties with good academic experience. Most of the faculty members presented & published papers at several reputed National, International conferences and journals. The department is well equipped with 09 laboratories. Department is working on outcome base teaching-learning process. Department has excellent Student-Teacher ratio as per AICTE Norms. The Department has formed a student association CSITA and ACM Society chapter under which cultural & technical events are organize. Department has good number of Placement Summer Internship in Industries is provided to student by Department. Department provide collaborative initiative with Industry. Department has organized CRT, Soft Skill & Technical Skill development programs for students in coordination with T&P Department.

HOD's message

Welcome to the Department of Computer Science and Engineering GNIET, Nagpur. The Department started in 2008 with 120 intake and Mtech (CSE) Programme started in 2014 with intake 24. The Department has a vibrant student body numbering around 208. The Department offers several attractive industries internship programme and Add-on Programme for all the students. The Department is actively engaged in reserach activities in various reserach areas. Several of out aluni hold important positions in the industry and academia worlwide. Students have been recetly placed, both in India and abroad , in several leading national and international comapnies including Flipkart, IBM , Mircrosfot , Oracle , Samsung, TCS , Dhoot Infotech etc. In GNIET focus is also given to activities beyong academics for the students.

Program Educational Objectives

PEO1

High-Quality Engineering Design and Development Work: Graduates of the program will engage in the effective practice of computer science and engineering to identify and solve important problems in a diverse range of application areas.

PEO2

Real Life Problem Solving: To educate students with proficiency in core areas of Computer science & Engineering and related engineering so as to comprehend engineering trade-offs, analyze, design, and synthesize data and technical concepts to create novel products and solutions for the real life problems.

PEO3

Leadership: Graduates of the program will engage in successful careers in industry, academia and attain positions of importance where they have an impact on their business, profession, and community.

PEO4

Lifelong Learning: Graduates of the program will adapt to contemporary technologies, tools, and methodologies to remain at the frontier of computer science and engineering practice with the ability to respond to the need of a challenging environment.
