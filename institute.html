About The Institution____


Good education encompasses instilling moral, aesthetic, athletic and intellectual values in the citizens of tomorrow. GNIT Nagpur established in 2009 by GNES society is one of the top engineering colleges under RTMNU is approved by All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi and Director of Technical Education, Maharashtra and NAAC Accredited. The college has lush green surrounding which provides conducive environment for students to grow not only as good Engineers but as world class citizens too. The campus is situated in the vicinity of MIDC Kalmeshwar which is the heart of the industrial zone. Spread over a sprawling 25 acres, the natural form of the landscape has been preserved.

Education Society

Guru Nanak Education Society, Nagpur came into existence in 2006. The society is registered under minority act & granted under permanent minority status.

The benevolence of Guru Nanak Devji has inspired the society to work for education. It is formed to serve the people with the motto- “Education First”.

The society has gained trust of people and standard par excellence in a short span of time. It imparts quality education in the field of Engineering and Management .Guru Nanak Institute of Technology and Guru Nanak Institute of Engineering and Technology are such magnificent, prestigious and well established institutes in Nagpur under the banner of Guru Nanak Education Society. GNIET is one of the most nurtured branches of GNES, Nagpur, which is working with a mission of spreading engineering & management education among the students who belong to rural areas. GNIT is also special in regard to imparting education of engineering in core branches like mechanical, civil, electrical along with derived branches like electronics and telecommunication & computer science & Engineering.

Guru Nanak Education Society has become an integral part of educational environment in Nagpur. Basically it believes in the service of nations through dedicated accomplishment of its vision & mission.

The society runs independent and coeducational colleges. As a renowned institution, Guru Nanak Educational Society seeks distinction in the exploration and transmission of knowledge. GNES has become distinctive among other institutes in its commitment towards graduate & post graduate level of teaching.

The society provides financial aids and various scholarship program. This helps the students from poor economical backgrounds to afford educational program run by prestigious colleges of GNES.

Guru Nanak Educational Society ,Nagpur is headed by Sardar Navneet Singhji Tuli ,a renowned politician and social worker who is dedicated to light the lamp of education in the society and has proven his excellence in doing so by setting up different technical and management study centres. GNIT & GNIET are some prominent ventures running successfully under his guidance. GNES is a trusted brand in Nagpur today for imparting technical education.

Group of Institutions

The Guru Nanak Institutions, Nagpur, established in 2006, with a committed vision of ‘Education First’. Due to untiring struggles of the Founder Chairman, Sardar Navneet Singh Tuli, along with other visionaries, the Institute has earned a status of one of the most prominent and leading educational centre in the state of Maharashtra.

To attain highest “quality” in all aspects from academics to administration, the institute has initiated the quality awareness programme by making functional an Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC). GNI focuses on achieving the highest academic excellence. Learning methodology, quality in teaching, planning, and flexibility in working are the integral part of the culture and practices at GNI. Faculty members are motivated to undergo for research and consultancy services to promote research culture while students are encouraged to participate in Rotaract and NSS activities to improve quality of human life.

Administration

The management of Guru Nanak Institution is supported by the members of the Governing Body in designing strategies for achieving the vision and mission of the Institute as well as administration of the institution.

The Principal is the head of the Institution who implements the strategies formulated by the management with the help of Heads of Departments. The quality policy of the Institute is formulated by the Management with the inputs from the students, faculty, Principal, Head of Departments, members of the Governing Body, taking into account the current trends in employment and needs of the society.

The Management extends all support, in terms of funding, Human wealth and Infrastructure in order to implement the quality policy in every aspect of the functioning of the Institute. The Chairman regularly meets the Principal and Heads of Departments to review and discuss the strategies by which the set goals are attained and to ensure that the Institute is in the right track towards achieving its Vision.
