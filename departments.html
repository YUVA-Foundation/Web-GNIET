The Department of Computer Science & Engineering established in 2008, is well-equipped with state-of-the-art infrastructure. The state of art infrastructure includes latest configuration desktops organized in five different laboratories.

There is an internet facility and inter-connected by a 24 hours server. The Department has a distinction of consistently achieving above 95% results in the final year. Students are encouraged to appear in GATE, CAT, GRE and other competitive examinations which has resulted in increasing number of students clearing these exams.
